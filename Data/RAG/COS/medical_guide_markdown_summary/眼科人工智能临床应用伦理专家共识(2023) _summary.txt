The "Expert Consensus on the Ethics of Clinical Application of Artificial Intelligence in Ophthalmology (2023)" outlines ethical guidelines for the use of AI in diagnosing and treating eye diseases. Developed by ophthalmologists, AI experts, and medical ethicists, it addresses concerns about data usage, patient privacy, and medical professional responsibilities. Key requirements include transparent data management, accountability, and fairness in AI applications, along with a call for ongoing education and regulation to build trust in AI technologies in clinical practice.
