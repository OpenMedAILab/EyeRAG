The "Age-Related Visual Function and Eye Health Management White Paper" addresses the challenges of age-related visual decline, particularly in China's growing elderly population. It highlights the need for proactive eye health management to enhance the quality of life for older adults, detailing physiological changes in the eye that lead to visual impairment. The document emphasizes clinical assessments of visual function, advocates for integrating eye health into community healthcare systems, and promotes public education on the importance of early intervention. Overall, it serves as a guideline for healthcare professionals to effectively manage age-related eye health.
