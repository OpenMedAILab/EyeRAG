The "Chinese Expert Consensus on Laser Corneal Refractive Surgery for Correction of Refractive Errors with Age-Related Accommodation Deficiency" (2021) outlines best practices for individuals with age-related accommodation issues undergoing laser surgery. It emphasizes rigorous preoperative screening, personalized surgical plans, and tailored postoperative care to minimize reliance on reading glasses. The document specifies indications and contraindications for various surgical approaches and stresses the importance of comprehensive preoperative assessments. Postoperative management addresses complications like dry eye and corneal healing. The consensus aims to standardize practices and enhance visual outcomes, patient satisfaction, and safety.
