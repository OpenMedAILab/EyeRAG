The "Chinese Expert Consensus on the Diagnosis and Treatment of Contact Lens-Associated Dry Eye (2024)" addresses the rising prevalence of dry eye syndrome among contact lens wearers in China. Developed by specialists in corneal disease, pediatric ophthalmology, and optometry, it covers definitions, mechanisms, assessment, prevention, and treatment of the condition. Key points include the importance of tear film stability, the influence of different lens types on dry eye risk, and the necessity of proper lens care. The consensus advocates for comprehensive evaluation and management strategies to improve visual quality and user satisfaction while preventing dry eye complications.
