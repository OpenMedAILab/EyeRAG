The "Expert Consensus on the Treatment of Pediatric Ocular Trauma in China (2024)" provides guidelines for managing eye injuries in children, who account for 15-20% of ocular trauma cases. It addresses the complexities of diagnosing and treating these injuries due to children's underdeveloped eye structures and communication challenges. The consensus includes recommendations on injury classification, history taking, examination methods, and treatment protocols, emphasizing thorough history collection, age-appropriate examination techniques, and specific treatment strategies. It also highlights the need for improved safety education, timely medical responses, and enhanced primary healthcare capabilities to better manage pediatric ocular trauma.
