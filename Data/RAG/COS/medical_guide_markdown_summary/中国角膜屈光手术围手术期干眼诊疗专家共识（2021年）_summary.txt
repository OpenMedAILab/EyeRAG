The "Expert Consensus on the Diagnosis and Treatment of Dry Eye During the Perioperative Period of Corneal Refractive Surgery in China (2021)" highlights the rising incidence of corneal refractive surgeries and the common complication of dry eye. It stresses the need for standardized diagnosis and treatment to improve postoperative visual outcomes and patient satisfaction. The consensus details mechanisms, risk factors, diagnostic methods, and management strategies for dry eye, recommending preoperative assessments, patient education, and personalized treatments like artificial tears and anti-inflammatory medications. This document serves as a guideline for ophthalmologists to enhance dry eye management in corneal refractive surgery.
