The "Expert Consensus Statement on the Clinical Application of Yunnan Hongyao Capsule for the Treatment of Fundus Hemorrhage" provides guidelines for using this traditional Chinese medicine, known for its hemostatic and circulation-promoting properties, in treating fundus and conjunctival hemorrhage. Developed by a panel of experts, the consensus covers indications, dosage, adverse reactions, and precautions, emphasizing safe and effective usage. It aims to standardize treatment protocols and improve patient outcomes for healthcare professionals in China.
