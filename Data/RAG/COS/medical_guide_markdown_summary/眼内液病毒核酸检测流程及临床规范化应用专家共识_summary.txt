The Ophthalmic Laboratory Testing Subspecialty Group and the Expert Committee of Laboratory Diagnosis of Ocular Diseases in China have established a consensus on standardized procedures for intraocular virus nucleic acid testing. This consensus highlights the critical role of viral nucleic acid testing in diagnosing intraocular viral infections and guiding treatment. It provides protocols for sample collection, laboratory testing, and quality control, emphasizing laboratory standards and specific testing methods, including real-time quantitative PCR. The aim is to improve the accuracy of diagnosing and treating intraocular viral infections through standardized practices.
