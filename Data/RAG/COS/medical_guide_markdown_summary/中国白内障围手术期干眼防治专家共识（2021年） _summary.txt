The "Chinese Expert Consensus on the Prevention and Treatment of Dry Eye During the Perioperative Period of Cataract Surgery" (2021) provides guidelines for managing dry eye in cataract surgery patients. It highlights the need for preoperative evaluation and treatment of ocular surface conditions, protection during surgery, and postoperative care. Key recommendations include thorough assessments, use of artificial tears, careful surgical techniques, and effective patient communication to improve outcomes and satisfaction.
