The "Chinese Expert Consensus on the Standardized Construction of Dry Eye Clinical Centers" (2021) highlights the need for specialized dry eye clinics in China to improve diagnosis and treatment for the 300 million affected individuals. It outlines standardized construction processes, addressing personnel, equipment, and medication requirements, and provides guidelines for facility setup and drug inventory. The consensus emphasizes the roles of specialized medical staff and the importance of quality control systems to enhance patient care and promote standardized dry eye treatment nationwide.
