The "White Paper on Standard Fitting of Color Corneal Contact Lenses" provides guidelines for the safe use of color contact lenses in China, addressing their rising popularity. It stresses the need for proper fitting and care to avoid eye health issues due to varying product quality. Key recommendations include ensuring users have no significant eye symptoms, proper lens fitting and comfort, thorough assessments before selection, user education on handling, and regular check-ups. The paper aims to promote awareness and compliance for a safe experience with color contact lenses.
