The Shanghai Public Health Emergency Expert Group has issued a consensus on preventing and controlling infectious conjunctivitis, including acute hemorrhagic conjunctivitis, epidemic keratoconjunctivitis, and trachoma. In light of the COVID-19 pandemic, which has linked respiratory infections to conjunctivitis, the document calls for updated guidelines. It details the etiology, clinical features, and epidemiology of these infections, and provides recommendations for clinical management and emergency response in ophthalmology. Key measures include patient identification, monitoring, infection control, epidemiological investigation, and public education on hygiene practices to effectively manage outbreaks.
