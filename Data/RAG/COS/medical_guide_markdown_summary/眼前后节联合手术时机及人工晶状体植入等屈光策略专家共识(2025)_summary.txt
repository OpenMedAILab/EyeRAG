The "Expert Consensus on the Timing of Combined Anterior and Posterior Segment Intraocular Surgery, Intraocular Lens Implantation, and Related Refractive Strategies (2025)" outlines advancements in ophthalmic surgery, focusing on combined techniques for cataracts and other eye diseases. It emphasizes the need for precise preoperative ocular measurements and tailored intraocular lens (IOL) strategies to optimize postoperative outcomes. Key recommendations include comprehensive biological assessments, individualized IOL implantation approaches, and effective management of complications. The document also addresses the impact of various eye conditions on surgical results and provides guidelines for correcting refractive errors, aiming to enhance the success of combined surgeries and improve patient vision recovery.
