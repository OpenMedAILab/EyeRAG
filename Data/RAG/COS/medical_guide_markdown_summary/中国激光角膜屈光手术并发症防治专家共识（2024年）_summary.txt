The "Chinese Expert Consensus on the Prevention and Treatment of Complications of Laser Corneal Refractive Surgery (2024)" highlights the safety and efficacy of laser corneal refractive surgery in China, practiced for over 30 years. While severe complications are rare, effective management is essential for preserving vision and quality of life. Developed by the Cornea Group of the Chinese Medical Association, the consensus addresses the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of complications such as delayed epithelial healing, haze, and infections. It underscores the importance of preoperative assessments, standardized surgical procedures, and diligent postoperative care to enhance surgical outcomes and patient satisfaction.
