The "Expert Consensus on the Treatment of Lightless Eyes from Mechanical Eye Trauma in China" (2020) presents advancements in managing severe eye injuries that result in lightless vision. Developed by the Ophthalmology Trauma Group of the Chinese Medical Association, it highlights that about 30% of lightless eyes can achieve anatomical repair and regain some vision through improved surgical techniques. The consensus advocates for standardized protocols for surgical interventions, emphasizing timely treatment to enhance eye preservation. Key points include methods for identifying lightless eyes, recommendations for surgical protocols, the necessity for experienced surgeons, and guidelines for preoperative and postoperative care. The document aims to enhance outcomes for patients with severe mechanical eye injuries.
