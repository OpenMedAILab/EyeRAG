The "Expert Consensus on the Integrated Traditional Chinese Medicine and Western Medicine Treatment of Dry Eye" advocates for a combined approach to managing dry eye, a condition that impacts quality of life. It recommends integrating Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) therapies, such as herbal medicine and acupuncture, with Western treatments like eye drops. The consensus categorizes dry eye into types based on tear composition and functional abnormalities, providing specific treatment recommendations supported by clinical evidence. It also emphasizes prevention strategies, including eye care, emotional health, and diet. The goal is to enhance patient outcomes through a tailored, evidence-based treatment approach.
