The "Chinese Expert Consensus on Management of Ophthalmic Day Surgery" (2021) provides guidelines for developing and managing day surgery in ophthalmology in China, highlighting the suitability of such procedures due to shorter operation times and lower risks. Developed by various medical associations, the consensus aims to standardize practices, enhance quality, and ensure patient safety. It covers facility design, organizational management, surgical execution, and quality metrics, including patient satisfaction and complication rates. Additionally, it offers recommendations for managing day surgery during infectious disease outbreaks, emphasizing rigorous safety measures. Overall, the document serves as a comprehensive guide for effective ophthalmic day surgery implementation in China.
