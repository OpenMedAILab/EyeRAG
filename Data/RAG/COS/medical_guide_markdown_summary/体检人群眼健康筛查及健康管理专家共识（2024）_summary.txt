The "Chinese Expert Consensus on Eye Health Screening and Management for Health Check-up Populations (2024)" highlights the need for early diagnosis and intervention in eye diseases to enhance health and reduce societal costs. It recommends standardized eye health assessments, particularly for adults over 40, those with chronic diseases, and high myopia patients. The consensus advocates for regular eye examinations and the integration of technology and artificial intelligence to improve screening efficiency and accuracy, aiming to establish a comprehensive framework for eye health management and promote early detection and treatment.
