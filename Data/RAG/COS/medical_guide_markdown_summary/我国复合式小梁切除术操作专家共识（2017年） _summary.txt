The "Expert Consensus on Composite Trabeculectomy" (2017) by the Glaucoma Group of the Chinese Medical Association outlines essential surgical techniques for trabeculectomy, a key glaucoma treatment. It highlights the management of scleral flap suture tension to mitigate complications like low intraocular pressure and scarring. The use of removable sutures and antifibrotic agents, such as mitomycin C, has improved outcomes. The document details surgical steps and aims to standardize the procedure to enhance glaucoma treatment effectiveness in China.
