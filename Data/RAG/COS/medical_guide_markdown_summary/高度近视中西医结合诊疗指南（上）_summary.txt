The "Guidelines for the Diagnosis and Treatment of High Myopia Integrating Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine" address the increasing prevalence of high myopia, especially among youth in China. They define high myopia, establish diagnostic criteria, and recommend integrated treatment strategies combining traditional Chinese medicine (TCM) and Western practices. The guidelines cover clinical symptoms, diagnostic methods, and treatment options, including optical interventions, TCM therapies, and surgical procedures, aiming to standardize management and improve patient outcomes through evidence-based recommendations.
