The "Expert Consensus on the Surgical Procedure for Glaucoma Drainage Valve Implantation in China (2019, 2nd Edition)" provides standardized guidelines to enhance the quality of glaucoma drainage valve surgeries and reduce complications. Revised by the Glaucoma Group of the Chinese Medical Association's Ophthalmology Branch, it outlines specific surgical steps, improved techniques, and postoperative care. Key recommendations include thorough site assessment, appropriate anesthesia, and careful valve implantation. The consensus also addresses the management of intraoperative and postoperative complications, aiming to improve clinical skills and patient outcomes for ophthalmologists.
