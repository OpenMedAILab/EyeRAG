The "Experts Consensus on the Clinical Personalized Diagnosis and Treatment of Presbyopia (2025)" addresses the visual issue of presbyopia, which affects aging individuals. Developed by leading Chinese ophthalmology and optometry experts, it highlights the need for personalized treatment due to varying visual needs and health conditions. Key challenges include a large population in need of correction, lack of awareness about treatment, and the necessity to meet individual demands. The consensus outlines a comprehensive approach to diagnosis and treatment options—such as glasses, contact lenses, surgical interventions, and medications—while emphasizing ongoing clinical evaluation to improve patient satisfaction and visual quality. It aims to provide scientific guidance for effective presbyopia management.
