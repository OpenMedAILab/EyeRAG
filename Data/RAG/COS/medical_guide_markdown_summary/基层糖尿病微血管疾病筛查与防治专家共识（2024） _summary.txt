The "Expert Consensus on Screening and Prevention of Diabetic Microvascular Disease for Primary Care (2024)" provides guidelines for the early identification and management of diabetic microvascular diseases, including diabetic retinopathy, kidney disease, neuropathy, and cardiomyopathy. Developed by experts from the Chinese Society of Microcirculation and the Chinese Society of Endocrinology, it emphasizes early screening, comprehensive management, and tailored prevention strategies in primary care. The consensus outlines screening methods, risk factor management, and treatment protocols to reduce the incidence and progression of these diseases, improve patient quality of life, and lower healthcare costs, while also advocating for collaboration between primary care and specialized services.
