The "Clinical Practice Guideline of Integrative Chinese and Western Medicine for Myopia in Children" addresses the increasing prevalence of myopia in Chinese youth. Developed by top medical associations, it standardizes evidence-based diagnostic, preventive, and management strategies. Recommendations include monitoring eye health, acupuncture, electrical stimulation, optical interventions, and medication to delay myopia onset, prevent progression, and manage complications. This guideline serves as a resource for healthcare professionals and the public in myopia prevention and management.
