The "Chinese Expert Consensus on Keratoprosthesis Procedure (2021)" provides guidelines for artificial corneal transplantation to restore sight in patients with end-stage corneal blindness. Developed by the Cornea Group of the Chinese Medical Association, it emphasizes standardized practices for the safe use of domestic artificial corneas, including the collar button design and Miok keratoprosthesis. The consensus covers surgical indications, operational protocols, perioperative medication, and complication management, aiming to enhance clinical outcomes for patients in China.
