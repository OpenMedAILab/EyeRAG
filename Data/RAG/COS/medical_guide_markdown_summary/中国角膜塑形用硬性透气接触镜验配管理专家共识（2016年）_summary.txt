The "Expert Consensus on the Fitting Management of Rigid Gas Permeable Contact Lenses for Orthokeratology in China" (2016) provides guidelines for fitting and managing orthokeratology lenses to reshape the cornea and reduce myopia. It stresses the importance of qualified medical personnel, outlines the fitting process and patient eligibility, and emphasizes patient education and compliance. The document also covers lens care, potential adverse reactions, and the need for a safety monitoring system for corneal health, aiming to standardize practices and promote responsible orthokeratology development in China.
