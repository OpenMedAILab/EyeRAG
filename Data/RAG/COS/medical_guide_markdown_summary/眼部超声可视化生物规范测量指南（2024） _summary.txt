The "2024 Guidelines for Visualized Biological Measurement of Ocular Ultrasound," created by experts from Chinese medical associations and international organizations, aim to standardize ocular ultrasound techniques such as A-scan, B-scan, UBM, and CDFI. The guidelines outline indications, contraindications, and operational steps for each method, emphasizing consistent measurement standards to improve diagnostic reliability. This comprehensive resource is designed to enhance the precision of ocular ultrasound examinations and support personalized treatment for eye diseases.
