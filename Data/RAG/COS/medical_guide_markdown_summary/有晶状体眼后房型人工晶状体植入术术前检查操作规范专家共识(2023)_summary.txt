The "Expert Consensus on Preoperative Examination Protocol for Posterior Chamber Intraocular Lens Implantation in Phakic Eyes" outlines crucial preoperative assessments to enhance the safety and effectiveness of this eye surgery. Developed by a panel of experts, it emphasizes comprehensive eye examinations, including measurements of corneal thickness, curvature, anterior chamber depth, and pupil diameter, as well as tests for visual quality and dry eye conditions. The consensus aims to standardize evaluations in light of rapid advancements in refractive surgery techniques, ensuring better surgical outcomes and safety.
