The "Management Consensus on the Clinical Use of Rigid Gas Permeable Contact Trial Lens" provides guidelines for the safe and standardized use of RGPCL in China. It emphasizes the importance of licensed medical facilities, qualified personnel, and a clean trial environment. Key recommendations include strict protocols for patient screening, lens handling, hygiene, and post-trial care, as well as regular lens maintenance and disinfection. Ongoing training and monitoring are also advised to ensure compliance and enhance patient safety, ultimately promoting the healthy development of the contact lens industry in China.
