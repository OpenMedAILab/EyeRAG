The "Expert Consensus on Orthokeratology Lens Care (2024)" provides essential guidelines for the care and maintenance of orthokeratology lenses, which help slow myopia progression in children and adolescents. Key points include:

1. **Care Procedures**: A detailed routine for cleaning, disinfecting, and storing lenses, with recommendations for various care products.
2. **User Guidelines**: Instructions for lens wearers on hygiene, cleaning techniques, and storage, emphasizing regular checks and adherence to protocols.
3. **Professional Standards**: Guidelines for the care of trial lenses in clinical settings, focusing on strict disinfection to prevent contamination.
4. **Safety Precautions**: Advisories for users to seek professional help for discomfort and to follow cleaning instructions to ensure eye health.

The consensus aims to improve the quality of care for orthokeratology lenses, promoting their safe and effective use in myopia management.
