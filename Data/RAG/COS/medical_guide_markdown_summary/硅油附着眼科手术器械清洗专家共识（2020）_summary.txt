The "Expert Consensus on Cleaning Methods for Ophthalmic Surgical Instruments Attached to Silicone Oil (2020)" provides standardized cleaning procedures for ophthalmic instruments that contact silicone oil, developed by experts from the Chinese Ophthalmic Nursing Society and the Chinese Medical Association. Key recommendations include separating instruments during surgery, immediate residue removal, proper transport with moisture protection, and using a dedicated cleaning kit for pre-treatment. The consensus outlines a multi-step cleaning process, initial disinfection methods, appropriate drying techniques, effectiveness testing, and compliance with medical packaging and sterilization standards. The goal is to ensure effective cleaning and enhance patient safety in ophthalmic surgeries.
