The "Expert Consensus on Emergency Treatment of Ocular Trauma in China" (2019) establishes standardized protocols for managing eye injuries in medical institutions across China. It highlights the necessity of appropriate facilities, equipment, and trained personnel to ensure effective treatment. Key recommendations include designated emergency areas, a list of essential medications, systematic diagnostic and treatment protocols, special considerations for pediatric care, and management strategies for common eye injuries. The consensus aims to enhance the quality of emergency care for ocular trauma and improve patient outcomes.
