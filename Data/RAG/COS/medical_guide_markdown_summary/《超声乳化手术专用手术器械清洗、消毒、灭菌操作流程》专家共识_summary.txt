The document outlines expert consensus guidelines for cleaning, disinfection, and sterilization of surgical instruments used in phacoemulsification surgery, developed by the Ophthalmic Nursing Committee of the Chinese Nursing Association. It addresses inconsistencies in practices across 64 medical institutions and includes key steps: immediate post-surgery cleaning, proper collection and transport, detailed cleaning procedures, disinfection with 75% ethanol, air drying, quality monitoring, safe packaging, and high-pressure steam sterilization. The consensus aims to standardize practices to improve safety and effectiveness in cataract surgeries.
