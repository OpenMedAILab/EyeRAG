The "Expert Consensus on Standardized Preoperative Visual Function and Imaging Examination for Vitreoretinal Surgery (2024)" provides essential protocols for preoperative assessments in vitreoretinal surgery. Developed by a collaborative expert group, it highlights the importance of thorough examinations to guide surgical decisions and planning. The document details various imaging techniques, including ultra-widefield fundus imaging, fluorescein angiography, OCT, and electrophysiological tests, emphasizing their methodologies and clinical relevance. The consensus aims to enhance medical quality, optimize resource use, and protect patient rights by standardizing procedures, serving as a comprehensive guide for ophthalmic surgeons to improve the safety and success of surgeries.
