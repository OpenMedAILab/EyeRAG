The 2019 "Expert Consensus on Standardization of Testing Equipment and Settings in the Screening for Myopic Prevention and Control in Children and Adolescents" provides guidelines for myopia screening in schools, focusing on quality control. It emphasizes the use of standardized visual acuity charts and automatic refractometers, detailing calibration, environmental settings, and data collection methods. The consensus aims to improve data accuracy and reliability, support an information management system for screening data, and promote consistency through standardized forms for recording student information and test results.
