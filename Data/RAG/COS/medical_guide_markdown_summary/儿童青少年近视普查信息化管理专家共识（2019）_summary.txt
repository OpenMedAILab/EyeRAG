The "Consensus on Information Management of Myopia Screening in Children and Adolescents (2019)" provides standardized procedures for myopia screenings in China, developed by experts from the Chinese Optometric Association and the Chinese Ophthalmological Society. It highlights the role of information technology in accurate data collection, secure storage, and efficient transmission. Key features include a unique identification system for participants, a streamlined four-step screening process, strict data security measures, and scalable data analysis for tracking myopia trends. The document also includes structured data tables, guidelines for ethical data use, and statistical methods for evaluating myopia prevalence and prevention strategies.
