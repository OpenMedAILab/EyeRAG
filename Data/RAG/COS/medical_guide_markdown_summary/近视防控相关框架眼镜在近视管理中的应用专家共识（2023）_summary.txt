The "Expert Consensus on the Application of Spectacle Related to Myopia Prevention and Control in Myopia Management (2023)" addresses the rising myopia rates among children and adolescents in China, emphasizing the need for effective prevention strategies. It identifies specially designed optical glasses as a primary solution, highlighting their convenience and minimal side effects. The document reviews various optical mechanisms for myopia control and evaluates the effectiveness of different corrective lenses. It also outlines guidelines for the use, fitting, and follow-up of these glasses, advocating for evidence-based practices and ongoing research to improve myopia management and enhance eye health in the youth population.
