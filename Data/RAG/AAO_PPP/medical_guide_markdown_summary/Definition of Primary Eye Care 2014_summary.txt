The American Academy of Ophthalmology underscores the significance of primary eye care, which offers accessible services for vision health. Ophthalmologists, as primary care providers, deliver essential services like screenings and referrals while emphasizing coordinated care that integrates eye health with general medical services. The policy outlines the necessary competencies for primary eye care physicians and reaffirms the vital role of ophthalmologists in providing high-quality eye care, having been approved multiple times since 1995.
