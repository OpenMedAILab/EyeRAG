The "Information Statement on On-Call Compensation for Ophthalmologists" highlights the challenges in delivering timely emergency eye care due to specialist shortages and limited hospital resources. It advocates for hospitals to compensate ophthalmologists for on-call services, alongside patient payments, and stresses the importance of collaboration between the two to enhance emergency care. The statement acknowledges the complexities of compensation arrangements and encourages ophthalmologists to work with local hospitals to improve access to emergency eye care.
