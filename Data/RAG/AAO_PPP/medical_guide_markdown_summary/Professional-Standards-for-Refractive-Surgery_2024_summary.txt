The "Professional Standards for Refractive Surgery" document provides essential guidelines for surgeons and healthcare professionals to ensure safe and effective patient care in refractive surgery. It emphasizes the surgeon's responsibility for patient safety and incorporates new recommendations from the 2024 Good Medical Practice. Key areas include maintaining competence, ensuring safety and quality, conducting surgeries in accredited facilities, clear communication and informed consent, continuity of care, accurate record keeping, teamwork, and maintaining trust. The document aims to enhance the quality and safety of refractive surgery practices, with regular reviews planned for ongoing relevance.
