The American Academy of Ophthalmology's "Information Statement" highlights the emergence of innovative diagnostic technologies for eye diseases, driven by smartphones and the Internet. These tools enhance patient assessment and enable healthcare providers to capture eye images affordably. The Academy stresses their role in improving patient understanding, access to care, and cost reduction, while outlining ethical responsibilities for ophthalmologists to prioritize patient welfare. Recommendations include evaluating new technologies for FDA approval, reliability, patient risk, and data security, while considering potential medicolegal liabilities.
